Diploma Management Courses List

Diploma in Management is a full-time diploma program conducted for a duration of a minimum of 1 year to a maximum of 2 years. The course gives the students an insight into the management skills and abilities needed for management activities and business.

The eligibility for pursuing this course is having earned a graduate degree with an aggregate of 50% marks in any discipline along with a minimum experience of 1 year. The curriculum is covered in 4 semesters, the examination of which is held in a span of every 6 months.
